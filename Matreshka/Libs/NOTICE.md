# Third-party libraries

The libraries below are embedded **unmodified** and remain under their own original
licenses. They are the property of their respective authors, not of the Matreshka
add-on. Refer to each library's own project page for the exact license text.

- **LibStub** — minimal versioning stub for World of Warcraft add-on libraries.
  Released into the public domain by its authors.
  https://www.wowace.com/projects/libstub

- **LibUIDropDownMenu** — a standalone, taint-free reimplementation of the Blizzard
  UIDropDownMenu, by Ketho. Distributed under its original license.
  https://github.com/Ketho/LibUIDropDownMenu
